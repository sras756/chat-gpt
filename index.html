Can you explain how neural networks work in simple terms?

Certainly! Think of a neural network like a team of people solving a puzzle together:

  1. Each person (neuron) looks at a small piece of information
  2. They share what they notice with others
  3. Together they combine these observations
  4. Gradually they work toward solving the whole puzzle

The network "learns" by adjusting how much attention to pay to each person's observations based on what helps solve similar puzzles correctly in the past.
